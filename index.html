About Netra

Netra is a cutting-edge facial recognition app that lets you compare faces securely and privately. All processing happens on your device, and no personal data is ever collected or shared.

  • Compare faces using your camera or photo library
  • Works completely offline — your data stays private
  • Simple, fast, and easy-to-use interface

Getting Started

To use Netra, you will need to allow the following device permissions:

Camera Access

We use the camera only when you choose to take a photo for facial comparison. The image stays on your device and is never shared.

Photo Library Access

We use the photo library only if you select an existing photo for comparison. Your photos remain private and never leave your device.
