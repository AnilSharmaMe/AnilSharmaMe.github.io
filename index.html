How Netra Works

 

Netra lets you compare faces quickly and securely.

 
       
  • Scan Faces: Use your camera or select a photo to scan for faces.
    •    
  • Private Vault: Save faces to a private, on-device list for comparison.
    •    
  • Local Processing: All facial processing and comparison happen on your device — nothing is uploaded.
    •    
  • Fast & Private: Instant results, with no personal data collected.
    •  
 

Permissions Required:

 
       
  • Camera Access: To scan a face from the live camera feed or take a new photo for your Vault.
    •    
  • Photo Library Access: To select an existing photo for scanning or for adding to your Vault.
    •  
 

See Netra in Action

 
   

1. Start a Scan

   

The main screen lets you instantly **Scan from Camera** or **Scan from Photos** to begin a comparison.

     
 
   

2. Review and Compare

   

After selecting or taking a photo, review it on the **Photo Review** screen, then tap **Compare Faces**.

     
 
   

3. Check for Face Detection

   

Netra will check the photo for a face. If one isn't detected, a notification like **No face detected** will appear.

     
 
   

4. Manage Your Private Vault

   

Tap the **Vault** icon to manage your list of saved faces. You can **Take Photo** or **Select from Photos** to add a new person for detection.
