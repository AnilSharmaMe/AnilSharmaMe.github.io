How Netra Works

Netra lets you compare faces quickly and securely.

  • Compare Faces: Use your camera or select a photo from your library to start comparisons.
  • Local Processing: All processing happens on your device — nothing is uploaded.
  • Multiple Categories: Compare faces from unsolved cases, wanted persons, or retail suspects.
  • Fast & Private: Instant results, with no personal data collected.

Permissions Required:

  • Camera Access: To take a photo for comparison.
  • Photo Library Access: To select an existing photo for comparison.
