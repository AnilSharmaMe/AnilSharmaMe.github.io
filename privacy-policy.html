Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 14, 2025

Introduction

Spotter App (“we”, “our”, or “us”) values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your information when you use the Spotter mobile application.

Information We Collect

  • Account & Device Information: Information you provide such as email address, device identifiers, and app-specific identifiers.
  • Location Data: Approximate or precise location data used to enable location-based alerts and mapping features.
  • Media: Images or photos that you voluntarily upload, capture, or choose to save within the app.
  • Usage Data: Diagnostic and usage information such as app interactions, feature usage, and crash logs.

Face Data & Biometric Information

What Face Data Is Collected

The app processes face-related data only when you explicitly choose to save a face or when a face image is processed for comparison purposes. This data may include:

  • Cropped face images derived from user-selected photos or incident alert images
  • Numerical face embeddings (vector representations) generated on-device

The app does not collect names, real-world identities, biometric identifiers, or government-issued identification.

How Face Data Is Used

Face data is used exclusively to provide core app functionality, including:

  • Allowing users to maintain a private face vault
  • Performing on-device face similarity comparisons
  • Displaying saved face images back to the user
  • Enabling users to manage or delete saved faces

Face data is not used for advertising, marketing, analytics, profiling, or tracking.

On-Device Processing

All face detection, alignment, and embedding generation is performed entirely on the user’s device. Face data is not processed in the cloud.

Data Storage & Retention

Face images and derived face data are stored locally on your device within the app’s secure, sandboxed storage area.

  • Face data is retained only for as long as you keep it
  • You may delete individual faces or all saved face data at any time within the app
  • Deleting the app permanently removes all stored face data

Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or share personal data or face data with third parties.

Face data is never transmitted to external servers and is not accessible by third-party service providers. We may disclose information only if required by law or to protect legal rights.

Security of Your Information

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect your information. All locally stored data benefits from iOS system-level encryption and sandboxing. However, no system can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

Your Rights & Choices

  • Access, review, or delete your stored information through the app
  • Delete saved face data at any time
  • Uninstall the app to permanently remove all stored data
  • Request information about your data by contacting us

Children’s Privacy

Spotter App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal or face data from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
