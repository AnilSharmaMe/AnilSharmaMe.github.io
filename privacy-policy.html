Netra App Privacy Policy

Transparency and your privacy matter to us

Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how the Netra app handles user data and device permissions.

Information We Collect

The Netra app does not require account registration and does not collect contact details, browsing history, or financial information. However, in order to provide the appâ€™s primary functionality (facial comparison), we process the following data:

Photos and Videos

If you choose to take a new photo with the camera or select one from your photo library, the image is used solely for comparison. These photos are stored only on your device and are never uploaded, transmitted, or shared outside your device.

Biometric Data

The app analyzes the photo locally to detect and compare facial features. This biometric information is processed entirely on your device and is never sent to external servers.

Face Data Retention

The Netra app does not retain any face data. All face detection and comparison are performed locally on your device. No face data is stored, uploaded, transmitted, or shared outside your device. Once the comparison is complete, the data remains fully under your control.

Use of Device Permissions

Camera Access: Used only when you choose to capture a new photo for comparison.

Photo Library Access: Used only when you select an existing photo for comparison.

Both permissions are optional and under your control. The app will request them only at the moment you use those features.

Third-Party Services

The app does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No personal data is ever shared with third parties.

Data Security

All photos and biometric data are stored only on your device. Since no personal data is transmitted or stored on external servers, the risk of unauthorized access is minimized.

Your Privacy Choices

You may choose not to grant camera or photo library access. In that case, some features of the app will not be available, but you can still use the app without providing photos.
