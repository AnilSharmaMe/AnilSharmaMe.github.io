Netra App Privacy Policy

Transparency and your privacy matter to us

Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how the Netra app handles user data and device permissions.

Information Collection

The Netra app does not collect, store, or share any personal information. We do not track your activity or require registration to use the app.

Use of Device Permissions

Camera Access: We use the camera only when you choose to take a photo for facial comparison. The image stays on your device and is never shared.

Photo Library Access: We use the photo library only if you select an existing photo for comparison. Your photos remain private and never leave your device.

Third-Party Services

The app does not use third-party analytics or advertising tools. If any external services are introduced in the future, this policy will be updated accordingly.

Data Security

Since no personal data is collected, there are no risks of unauthorized access to your personal information from our app.
